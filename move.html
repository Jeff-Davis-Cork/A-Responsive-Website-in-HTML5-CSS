Packing Up: International Shipping

A learning experience

Apparently, the best way to pack up all your stuff (if you have enough) is to put it on pallets and load it up on a ship.

The shipping company I used in America told me that shipping was done by weight, not size. Big mistake.

I ended up paying twice what I should have. I know this as I first packed up my stuff for Australia and then re-packed it for Ireland. The Australian company told me about shipping by size!
